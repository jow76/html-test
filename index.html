Red Panda Facts

Red Pandas were the first ever Pandas!

As far as we have managed to discover, Red Pandas are the longest living type of panda in the history of planet Earth. They survived this long by being really cool.

Red Pandas sleep for 2/3 of the day!

Despite only having a lifespan of 9-13 years, red pandas are still more than willing to sacrifice 2/3 of their precious time in the name of napping.

They are the smallest panda in the world!

As if they weren't adorable enough, red pandas also happen to be the smallest of their kind This is key to their survival as no one has the heart to kill them.

They don't just eat bamboo!

While bamboo consists of a great majority of their diet, red pandas also eat eggs, flowers, birds and small rodents!

What's a red panda?

Red pandas are my favourite animal that actually exist. They are found in high-altitude forests in Asian countries such as Nepal and Myanmar.

Types of red panda

There are two different types of red pandas:

  • Himalayan
  • Chinese

The Chinese subspecies is usually larger and their fur has a deeper red colour. Their tails are also more defined, where the red is darker and the white is lighter.
